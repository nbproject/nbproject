Contact the NB Team

NB started as (and still partly is) a research project at MIT. In 2012, it began to transition to a community-supported project based at http://nbproject.org. Consequently, NB is provided as-is, without any form of warranty. This means, among other things, that NB doesn't have a dedicated support team.

However, it has a great community... So if you have questions about NB, please use our forum. You can also check our FAQ.

As a last resort, you can contact the NB team at nb-team@csail.mit.edu. However, please be aware that your questions will likely be answered much faster if you post them on our forum.
