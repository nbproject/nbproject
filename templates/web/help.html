NB Help and Tutorial (work in progress)

NB is a web-based forum that lets you collaborate on top of any PDF document. If you have questions about NB, please visit our FAQ and our forum.

Readers and Students...

Navigating across a PDF and its discussions

  • Use your mouse or the and keys to move from discussion to discussion.
  • Use your mouse or the and keys to scroll up and down the document.

Authoring Posts

  • Drag you mouse across any region on the pdf to create a new discussion on top of that region
  • To post a reply: Click on the Reply link right next to the comment that you wish to reply to, or right-click on that comment and choose "Reply" from the context menu.

Teaching staff...

To learn more about using NB with teaching, check out the teaching staff tutorial.
